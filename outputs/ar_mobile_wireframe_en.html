This entry handles how the app runs and account security. It does not edit nickname, avatar, AR card, or other personal display content.

Potentially Missed Product Points

  • AR permissions and onboarding: first launch needs camera permission, motion sensor permission, and spatial scanning guidance.
  • Recognition states: no plane detected, scanning, ready to place, tracking lost, light too low, or too close.
  • Content entry: the add action can include characters, tasks, props, QR scanning, or imported links.
  • Action button: the main UI only shows a clean dot. Expanded actions such as adding objects, recording, and shooting should be explained in onboarding.
  • Bottom segmented control: Explore, Action, and Me belong to one mode-switching group and should be framed in one shared container.
  • Explore entry: the left entry discovers nearby content, recommended tasks, and scannable interactive objects. It no longer handles records.
  • Status toast: hidden by default, briefly shown for spatial recognition, loading resources, permission issues, or tracking loss.
  • Focus adjustment: use a temporary radial control triggered by holding above the action button and sliding left or right; release to dismiss.
  • Anchor management: placed content needs rename, move, hide, delete, and relocalize actions.
  • Safe exit: when leaving AR, ask whether to save the current placements and records to avoid accidental loss.
  • Weak network and offline states: character assets, task config, and history need loading and retry states.
  • Privacy: the Me area should include privacy settings, data clearing, and Photos/Location/Camera permission management.

Suggested MVP Flow

Open ARAuthorize camera and enter scanning state.
Recognize SpaceShow placeable areas and subtle guidance.
Add ContentSelect and place a character, task, or prop.
Record / ManageCapture and save, then return to Me to view tasks.
  • Interaction priority: keep only AR objects and necessary prompts in the center; keep tool buttons collapsed toward the edges by default.
  • More can contain flash, grid, sound, tutorial, and feedback to reduce pressure on the main view.
  • The Me page can start with three blocks: profile info, custom character, and placed tasks. Achievements and shop can come later.
